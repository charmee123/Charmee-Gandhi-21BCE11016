Advanced Chess Like Game




    Piece Movement Guide

  • Pawns (P):Move one step in any directions (⬆⬇⬅➡).
  • Hero 1 (H1): Move 2 steps in any direction(⬆⬇⬅➡).
  • Hero 2 (H2): Move two steps diagonally (↖↗↙↘).
  • For player A: F works as B and B works as F so for moving ahead press B.
  • At the end if only player B remains then player B is winner vice versa.
